About Us

Numr is Africa's leading real estate data and analytics platform. We're on a mission to make property investment decisions more accessible through comprehensive market intelligence and analytics.

The Role

We're seeking a Real Estate Research Analyst to lead our market research initiatives and develop actionable insights for property investors. This role combines deep real estate knowledge with data analysis skills to produce market-leading intelligence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead property market research and analysis for Nigerian real estate markets
  • Develop and maintain standardized property valuation methodologies
  • Create weekly market insights and monthly trend analysis reports
  • Build relationships with industry stakeholders and data providers
  • Design and implement real estate data collection frameworks
  • Contribute to our weekly newsletter with market insights
  • Create property market forecasts and investment recommendations

Required Skills & Experience

  • 3+ years experience in real estate research, valuation, or analytics
  • Strong understanding of Nigerian property markets
  • Excellent data analysis and visualization skills
  • Proficiency with Excel and data analysis tools
  • Outstanding written and verbal communication
  • Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics, Finance, or related field

Preferred Qualifications

  • RICS or local real estate professional qualification
  • Experience with Python, R, or similar data analysis tools
  • Previous work with property data platforms or PropTech companies
  • Published research or market reports
  • Master's degree in relevant field

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and equity options
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Professional development support
  • Collaborative, fast-paced startup environment
